Privacy & Cookie Policy

This page explains the privacy and cookie approach of this site. In short: it is a simple, static personal blog and it does not track you.

What data is processed?

There are no sign-ups, forms or accounts here; we do not collect your personal data. The site runs only on the technical requests needed to show you the pages.

Cookies and local storage

No advertising or tracking cookies are used. Only your browser's local storage is used to remember:

  • Theme preference (light/dark) — so you see the same theme on reload.
  • Cookie-notice acknowledgement — so the notice is not shown again.

This data stays in your browser only; it is not sent to us or third parties. You can clear it anytime from your browser settings.

Third-party services

Fonts are loaded via Google Fonts and the site is hosted on GitHub Pages. These providers may keep access logs (e.g. IP) for technical reasons. There are no analytics or ad scripts.
