Flagship Product

İçtiHub — AI-powered legal platform

A platform designed for lawyers, legal counsel and the legal departments of organizations, bringing together case-law search, document analysis and a smart legal assistant. Behind it is EcoFluxion's own language model, fine-tuned for the Turkish legal language.

  • Advanced case-law search (semantic)
  • Document analysis and a smart legal assistant
  • Enterprise-grade secure cloud infrastructure

Areas of Expertise

The technologies he works on

The engineering behind the products. All of it serves a single purpose: accurate, fast and trustworthy Turkish artificial intelligence.

Large Language Models (LLM)

He trains and ships his own models, fine-tuned for the Turkish legal language, into production.

Natural Language Processing

Understanding Turkish and multilingual text: classification, inference and meaning-based processing.

RAG — Retrieval-Augmented Generation

A retrieval architecture that stops the model from “making things up” and grounds answers in real documents.

Semantic Search & Vector DB

MCP Agent Systems

Agent architectures that use tools and route intelligently via the Model Context Protocol.

Model Training & MLOps

Training, fine-tuning and continuous deployment on large-scale infrastructure. Experience with TRUBA and HPC.
