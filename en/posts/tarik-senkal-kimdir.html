Who is İsmail Tarık Şenkal?

TŞ
İsmail Tarık Şenkal
EcoFluxion · Co-Founder
· 6 min read

İsmail Tarık Şenkal, or simply Tarık Şenkal, is an engineer and entrepreneur determined to make artificial intelligence genuinely useful in everyday life. Today, under the roof of EcoFluxion Teknoloji A.Ş., where he is a co-founder, he develops AI products focused on Turkish.

“I want to turn AI from a presentation slide into a product people open every single day.”

In brief

  • Who: AI engineer and entrepreneur.
  • What he does: Builds products with large language models (LLMs), natural language processing, and RAG architectures.
  • Where: Ankara, METU Technopolis (ODTÜ Teknokent).
  • Company: EcoFluxion Teknoloji A.Ş. — co-founder.
  • Flagship product: İçtiHub, an AI platform for legal professionals.

From engineering to entrepreneurship

Like that of most technology founders, Tarık Şenkal's story begins with a single curiosity: the question of whether computers can truly understand language. Over time, he focused his interest in software and artificial intelligence on natural language processing (NLP) — that is, the challenge of getting machines to read, understand, and produce text.

That interest carried him into large-scale AI work. Through TÜBİTAK events, Turkey's national high-performance computing infrastructure TRUBA, and European-level programs such as EUMaster4HPC, he gained hands-on experience in how models are trained and put into production on large infrastructures. This period shaped the technical DNA of the company he would later found.

Why does it matter? Training large language models is neither cheap nor easy; it demands serious computing infrastructure and engineering discipline. Tarık Şenkal's early exposure to these infrastructures laid the foundation for EcoFluxion becoming a company that “trains its own model.”

Founding EcoFluxion

In 2024, he launched EcoFluxion Teknoloji A.Ş. as a co-founder at METU Technopolis in Ankara. The company's starting point is clear: to build AI products that genuinely understand Turkish and that are reliable and useful. EcoFluxion is not a “consultancy” shop; it is an R&D company that builds its own products.

The first concrete outcome of this vision was İçtiHub: a platform that brings together case-law search, document analysis, and a smart legal assistant for lawyers and legal departments. Behind it lies EcoFluxion's own language model, fine-tuned for Turkish legal language — MevzuatBot.

What does he believe?

A few core ideas guide Tarık Şenkal's work:

  • Turkish deserves the AI it has been waiting for. Most powerful models are designed around English; everyone who speaks Turkish should have access to the same quality in their native language.
  • Accuracy matters above all else. Especially in fields like law, systems that don't “make things up” and that ground their answers in real documents are essential. That is why RAG architecture sits at the heart of the work.
  • Research should become a product. A paper or a prototype is valuable; but the real value lies in turning it into a product used by thousands of people.

In short: İsmail Tarık Şenkal is a founder working to bring Turkish AI out of the lab and into real life. If you'd like to dig deeper, you can continue with the articles on what EcoFluxion is or on how large language models work.
