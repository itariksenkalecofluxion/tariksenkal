What is İçtiHub?

TŞ
İsmail Tarık Şenkal
EcoFluxion · Co-Founder
· 7 min read

İçtiHub is EcoFluxion's flagship product: an AI-powered legal platform built for legal professionals. Its goal is simple yet ambitious — to turn the case-law searches, document reviews and research that consume a lawyer's hours into a matter of minutes.

If you would like to experience the platform for yourself, you can reach İçtiHub directly at ictihub.com.

"İçtiHub's job isn't to replace the lawyer; it's to give back the most tedious, most time-consuming work."

What problem does it solve?

In law, the right decision often comes down to finding the right precedent. But locating what you are looking for among the millions of decisions across the Court of Cassation (Yargıtay), the Council of State (Danıştay) and other sources is both time-consuming and prone to oversights. İçtiHub takes on that burden: it understands a question asked in natural language, retrieves the relevant decisions, and summarizes them along with their reasoning.

Core features

  • Case-law search: Not keyword-based, but meaning-based (semantic) search. It understands "what you mean."
  • Document analysis: It reads and summarizes contracts, petitions and decisions, and flags risky points.
  • Smart legal assistant: It answers your questions and links them to sources; it doesn't "make things up," it grounds them in fact.
  • Archive and document management: Legal sources and institutional documents in one place.
  • Enterprise security: Secure cloud infrastructure, multi-user collaboration.
Who is it for? Lawyers, law firms, corporate legal counsel offices, and public or private organizations with legal departments. In short, anyone who works with case law and documents.

What's behind the scenes?

İçtiHub is no "magic"; it rests on a solid engineering stack:

  1. MevzuatBot — A large language model trained for Turkish legal language. It understands legal terminology.
  2. RAG — It grounds answers in real decisions and legislation, thereby reducing the AI's risk of "hallucination."
  3. Semantic search + vector database — Fast, meaning-based retrieval across millions of documents.
  4. MCP agent system — It intelligently directs which tool should be used and when.

If you are curious about how these components come together, the what is a large language model and what is RAG articles are a good place to start.

Why does it matter?

Turkish law carries a language and logic all its own. General models designed for English often stumble in this domain. That is precisely where İçtiHub's value lies: it has been built specifically for Turkish and for legal language. That means both accuracy and trust — the most critical thing in a field like law, where the margin for error must be low.
